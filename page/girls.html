Girls in Computer science

This the first project I did in my master degree. It is not a project requiring coding. The reason I did it is purely out of curiosity. I had a brother who works as an engineering in China, but I had no intrests on it at that time. In high school, I liked to study history, literature, then I chose to study Journalism as my undergrauate degree. Computer science used to seem very far from me. However, after I admitted by this major and started to learn it, I found it fascinating and sometimes like magic. Gender imbalance is a long time issue in Tech industry. So when I became fond of it, I wanted to do a little research about it and I also liked to know other girls' thoughts about Computer science.

I did some infographics, a timeline regarding female in computer science, some interviews.
