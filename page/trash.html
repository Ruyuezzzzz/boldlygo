Map the trash

This is a team project made by other students in our major and I in Hack the local. It is an event held by our shcool to bringing together journalism students, community journalists, software developers and local government representatives to create data-driven solutions to news challenges.

Our group dealt with the trash problem in Cardiff. Combining social media and map, it is easy to target which place need to be clean just as you send a photo with the hash tag "#htl16_litter" and attach the precise location. Then it will be showed on the map. We also did a heatmap to display the littering condition in Cardiff. This site is aimed to help the community with the trash problem.
